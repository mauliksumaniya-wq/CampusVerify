🚀 Quick Start Guide:

  1. Get Session ID & Code from your teacher.
  2. Ensure phone GPS / Location is turned ON.
  3. Click the 🎓 Student Portal button above.
  4. Enter codes and click on 🛰️ Verify.
  5. Please wait while the system identifies your location.
  6. Once identified, select your name from the list and click confirm.
🛡️

Safe & Secure: This app cannot save or share your live location with anyone. It only calculates the distance to college to verify your presence.
